Welcome to l'Immobilière de l'Estuaire (Estuaire Real Estate Agency), an independent agency offering private and commercial real estate services, including purchasing and selling houses, apartments and buildable land.

Based in Bordeaux, our Agency covers four (5) real estate local markets: (1) Bordeaux, (2) Communauté Urbaine de Bordeaux (C.U.B.) - Bordeaux Township, including all areas between Blanquefort and Léognan, and from Cestas through Carbon-Blanc, (3) the « Entre-Deux Mers » region, north of Bordeaux, (4) the Libournais region, including all townships alongside the Dordogne River and highway A89, and finally (5) the Blayais region, from Blaye to St André de Cubzac, including Bourg sur Gironde and Pugnac.
