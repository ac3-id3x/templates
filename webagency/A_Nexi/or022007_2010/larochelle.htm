Depuis plus de 60 ans notre cœur
bât pour La Rochelle

Généreuse et belle, traversée par 1000 ans de traditions, de cultures d’histoire, d’architecture et d’aventuriers La Rochelle est une ville dont tout le monde parle avec délice et envie. Avec un patrimoine extraordinaire, son port, ses rues piétonnes, ses monuments, La Rochelle est une ville dont on a du mal à se passer. Tournée vers l’avenir et l’innovation, La Rochelle est une ville soucieuse de son environnement. Bercée par un ensoleillement exceptionnel, la ville porte depuis longtemps de nombreux efforts sur la qualité de vie de ses habitants. Depuis plus de 60 ans, le cœur de Immobilier Desvallois n’a cessé de battre au rythme de la passion pour cette cité.



Le cabinet Desvallois travaille dans une zone couvrant une cinquantaine de kilomètres autour de La Rochelle. En 60 ans il a acquit une parfaite connaissance du patrimoine immobilier local et partage avec ses habitants l’enthousiasme et l’affection pour leur région.

Immobilier Desvallois is an established practice with a qualified Chartered Surveyor heading the partnership. We incorporate over 60 years of market experience with modern service industry philosophies to give our clients the highest quality of professional advice.

The philosophy

We believe that a number of attributes distinguish our firm from others.

Local Coverage
We are active throughout the area of La Rochelle with wide experience in all the principal market sectors. We are based in the historic town center nearby underground parking facilities.

The right size
We are small enough to provide service at the highest level to our clients, but large enough to cover fully markets in wich we operate.

Commitment
We are a profitable practice, which enables us to devote ours skills and energies to the interest of our clients, as well as investing in the latest market databases and information technology. We keep our clients fully informed at all times.

Integrity and professionalism
We are commited both to the ethics of our French Professional body the FNAIM, and our UK Professional body, the Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors.

The disciplines

Agency
Today’s marketplace is a dynamic environment which demands in-depht and first hand knowledge of occupational trends, availability and preferences. We act for both owners and occupiers in the acquisition, disposal and letting of vacant residential and commercial property.

Investisment
We act for property companies, institutional clients and individuals in the acquisition and disposal of investment in La Rochelle. Our day to day dealings in the occupational and agency markets give us first hand knowledge of tenant demand and trends in rental value.

Development
We have a track record in the sale of residential property development cycle, from the initial pre-planning stages through the sale of completed scheme. The service to developer includes identifying the optimum use of a site, site assembly and negociation, maximising the planning position with the help of the Service Technique of La Rochelle, market analyses and financial appraisals.

We believe that the formule for our success is placing the needs of our clients at the forefront. We exist to serve our clients.
