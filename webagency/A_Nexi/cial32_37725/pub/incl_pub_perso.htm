Lectoure in the Gers is a picturesque and historic town appreciated by tourists and connoisseurs for its historic architecture, art, culture, and cosmopolitan lifestyle. The Gers, renowned for its rolling landscapes, pretty stone properties and traditional gastronomy encapsulates all that is most engaging in this beautiful region of Gascony that remains one of the truly authentic French departments.

Lectoure has been the hub of Laurent Lameille’s property business for more than twenty years, making him one of the most experienced real estate specialists in the Gers and south west France. Whether you are buying or selling, wish to be advised on real estate investment or obtain an evaluation of your property, experience counts.

Listening to, accompanying and advising clients is the heart of our business. Laurent Lameille and his team of qualified bilingual advisors are at your service to ensure that you benefit from their combined experience and expertise in all types of property transactions.
