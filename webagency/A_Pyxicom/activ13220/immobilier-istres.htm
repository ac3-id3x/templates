  • L’agence travaille toujours avec des mandats inscrits sur le registre officiel des mandats et des bons de visite.

Why should you call an estate agent?

You are considering buying a property in France?
You are wondering how to do?

  • The estate agent is an adviser that will bring you his/her expertise and his/her knowledge of the real estate market
  • He/she will sort out the properties that meet exactly your demand and budget, so that you will not unnecessarily lose your time searching

Be careful!
Be really careful when choosing your estate agent!

There are some guarantees that every estate agent has to give you to prove you his/her reliability, professionalism and lawfulness.
So, before choosing him/her, make sure that:

  • The estate agency holds a professional card issued by the Préfecture to the manager of the agency.
  • The manager has the necessary qualifications for this job and is not an unskilled man of straw.
  • The estate agency holds a guarantee of payment issued by a bank in the name of the manager.
  • The estate agency always works with orders registered at the Official French Orders and Visit Bonds Register.
