Where French market-makers meet

With its various internet sites, the SeLoger.com group has become today the prime marketplace where supply meets demand for French real estate.The group’s main sites are :

  • www.seloger.com: whose purpose is to publish classified advertising for sales and rentals of real estate located in France, and to promote complementary on-line services to Web-users, such as financing, decorating, moving, insurance, etc. SeLoger.com is the leader in France in real estate classified advertising on the Internet;
  • www.immostreet.com: a platform for European real estate classified advertising -- for property located in France, Germany, Belgium, Spain, Italy, Israel, Portugal and Switzerland, with items searchable in nine languages;
  • www.selogerneuf.com: specializing in the sale of new real estate programs in France;
  • www.lacoteimmo.com: a fee-based, on-line real estate price guide


SeLoger.com provides highly valuable services to individuals looking for real estate, to real estate professionals who want to give unique visibility to their ads, and to advertisers wishing to take advantage of effective vehicles of communication.
