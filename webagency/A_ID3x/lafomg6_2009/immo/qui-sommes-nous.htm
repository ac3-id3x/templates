Qui sommes-nous ?

Une marque bientôt trentenaire

1981. A leur sortie de l'université, deux jeunes diplômés décident de s'associer pour créer une agence immobilière à Enghien-les-Bains, dans le nord de la région parisienne. Ils cherchent un nom pour leur agence. Ils le veulent sympathique, facile à mémoriser et à prononcer. Ils trouveront l'inspiration dans l'adresse de l'agence, située sur la route de laforêt de Montmorency. Ce sera Laforêt !

Le temps de recruter et de former ses premiers collaborateurs, de mettre au point ses méthodes de vente et de développer sa notoriété locale, l'agence de quartier est devenue une entreprise solide qui ouvre un second point de vente, puis un troisième, un quatrième et un cinquième. Puisque les techniques de vente forgées et expérimentées au sein des agences Laforêt fonctionnent, il est désormais nécessaire de fédérer les collaborateurs autour de trois valeurs qui servent de socle à la marque :

  • la clarté qui exige de fournir au client l'ensemble des informations nécessaires à sa décision ;
  • la convivialité qui instaure un vrai climat de confiance réciproque ;
  • l'efficacité qui fonde la relation sur le professionnalisme et la rigueur.

Si ces méthodes fonctionnent à l'échelle de cinq agences, pourquoi ne pas les faire appliquer par d'autres et viser 20, 50, 100 agences ? En 1991, dix ans après la création de la première agence immobilière à l'enseigne, le réseau de franchise Laforêt Immobilier est né. Dès lors, l'ascension est régulière.

Très rapidement, le cap des 100 agences est franchi, celui des 500 le sera en 2005. Vingt ans après la création du réseau de franchise, en 2011, Laforêt compte un peu plus de 750 agences à l'enseigne en France et en vise 1.000 pour l'année 2013. Aujourd'hui, nous sommes présents également à l'étranger (Portugal, Maroc, Luxembourg, ...). L'ambition de Laforêt est de devenir « la préférence de ses clients » : jour après jour, mois après mois, année après année, les agences franchisées de réseau renforcent leurs compétences au service des projets immobiliers de leurs clients.

WHO ARE WE ?

A THIRTY YEAR BRAND SOON

1981. Upon leaving the university, two graduates decide to join to create a real estate agency in Enghien-les-Bains, in the north of Paris. They search for a name for their agency. They want a friendly one, easy to remember and pronounce .They find inspiration in the address of the agency, located on the laforêt of Montmorency road. It will be Laforêt!

Just the time to recruit and train its employees first, to develop its sales methods and develop its local reputation, the neighborhood agency has become a solid company that opens a second store then a third, a fourth and a fifth . Since sales techniques forged and tested within agencies Laforet work, it is now necessary to unite employees around the three values which serve as the foundation for the brand:

  • Clarity which requires to provide the customer with all the necessary information for his own decision;
  • Friendliness that creates a real reciprocal trust atmosphere;
  • Efficiency that bases relationships on professionalism and rigor.

If these methods work across five agencies, why not enforce them to aim to other 20, 50, 100 agencies? In 1991, ten years after the creation of the first branded real estate agency, the Franchise network Laforêt Immobilier was born. Therefore the rise is regular.

Very quickly the number of 100 agencies is achieved, that of 500 will be in 2005. Twenty years after the creation of the Franchising network, in 2011, Laforet has just over 750 branches in France and it aims to 1000 for 2013. Today we are present abroad too (Portugal, Morocco, Luxembourg …).

Laforet’s ambition is to become “their clients ‘preference”: day after day, month after month, year after year, franchising network agencies strengthen their skills to the service of their customers ‘real estate projects.

The “Laforet” brand will soon be 30 years old.

Laforet was founded in 1981 by two university graduates in Enghien-les-Bains, north of Paris. The inspiration for the name of their new estate agency came from the forest which lies close by the rue Montmorency where their agency was located. They chose the name because it was friendly, easy to pronounce and to remember. So it became Laforet!

They soon set about recruiting and training their employees, developing their sales methods and establishing their reputation locally. It was not long before their agency expanded to become a network of first 2, then 3,then 4, then 5 agencies.

They discovered that the sales techniques they had tried and tested worked so well that they trained their employees in the three values which serve now as the foundation of the Laforet brand.

Transparency – providing all the necessary information so that the client can make his own decision.

A friendly approach - designed to create a relationship of trust.

Efficiency – delivering professional and disciplined working practices

Their methods worked so well for the first five agencies they set up that they realised they would work just as well for 20, 50, or 100. So in 1991, ten years after the creation of the first estate agency, the franchise network Laforet Immobilier was born and the brand has seen a steady rise ever since.

The number of franchises quickly grew to 100 agencies, then to 500 in 2005. In 2011, 20 years after the network was formed there were over 750 branches in France and aims soon to have 1000.

Today we have offices in Portugal, Morocco and Luxembourg…

As the network of franchises grows and the agencies strengthen their skills in the service of the clients real estate projects Laforet aims to be recognised as the client’s first choice day after day, month after month, year after year.

CHI SIAMO ?

Un marchio presto trentenne

1981. Dopo l’università due giovani laureati decidono di associarsi per creare un’agenzia immobiliare a Enghien-les-Bains, a nord della regione parigina. Cercano un nome per la loro agenzia. Lo vogliono simpatico, facile da memorizzare e da pronunciare. Trovano l’ispirazione nell’indirizzo dell’agenzia situato su route de laforêt de Montmorency. Sarà Laforêt!

Il tempo di assumere e di formare i suoi primi collaboratori, di mettere a punto i suoi metodi di vendita e di sviluppare una notorietà locale, l’agenzia del quartiere é diventata un’impresa solida che apre un secondo punto vendita, poi un terzo, un quarto e un quinto. Dato che le tecniche di vendita forgiate e sperimentate all’interno delle agenzie Laforêt funzionano, é ormai necessario trasmettere ai collaboratori tre valori che servono da colonna al marchio:

  • la chiarezza con lo scopo di fornire al cliente un insieme di informazioni necessarie per la sua decisione;
  • la cordialità che instaura un vero clima di fiducia reciproca;
  • l’efficacia che fonda le relazione su professionalità e rigore.

Se questi metodi funzionano in cinque agenzie, perché non farli applicare da altri e mirare a 20, 50, 100 agenzie ? Nel 1991, dieci anni dopo la creazione della prima agenzia immobiliare con questo marchio, nasce la rete in franchising Laforêt Immobilier. Da quel momento, l’ascesa é costante.

Molto rapidamente 100 agenzie fanno parte della rete in franchising, 500 nel 2005. Vent’anni dopo la creazione della rete in franchising, nel 2011, Laforêt conta poco più di 750 agenzie con la propria insegna e mira ad averne 1000 per l’anno 2013. Oggi siamo presenti anche all’estero (Portogallo, Marocco, Lussemburgo...). L’ambizione di Laforêt é quella di diventare la “preferenza dei suoi clienti” : giorno dopo giorno, mese dopo mese, anno dopo anno, le agenzie in franchising della rete rafforzano le loro competenze al servizio dei progetti immobiliari dei loro clienti.
